Pay Attention Dammit!

This is a 2 player reading comprehension game to help prepare for exams or just hone your reading prowess.

You'll be given a shore essay to read together, followed by 5 or 7 multiple choice questions. your buzzer is the A (for Awesome) key, your buzzer is the P (for Perfect) key. Whoever buzzes first will have 3 seconds to answer the question. If the answer is correct that player gets 1 point BUT if it is wrong you lose 1 point. If the player doesn't answer within 3 seconds the other player gets 3 seconds to answer. If both players fail to answer the question YOU BOTH LOSE 1 POINT!

Turn on your big brains and get your trigger fingers ready!
