Focus!

This is a 2 player reading comprehension game to help prepare for exams or just hone your reading prowess.

You'll be given a short essay to read together, followed by 5 or 7 multiple choice questions. your buzzer is the A (for Awesome) key, your buzzer is the P (for Perfect) key. Whoever buzzes first will have 3 seconds to answer the question. If the answer is correct that player gets 1 point BUT if it is wrong you lose 1 point. If the player that buzzed first doesn't answer within 3 seconds they lose a point! If neither player buzzes the question will time out and neither player gets a point.

Turn on your big brains and get your trigger fingers ready!
