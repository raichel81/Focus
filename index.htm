Focus!

This is a 2 player reading comprehension game to help prepare for exams or just hone your reading prowess.

You'll be given a short essay to read together, followed by 5 to 7 multiple choice questions. your buzzer is the A (for Awesome) key, your buzzer is the P (for Perfect) key. Whoever buzzes first will have whatever is remaining on the timer to answer the question. If the answer is correct that player gets 1 point BUT if it is wrong they lose 1 point. If neither player buzzes the question will time out and neither player gets a point.

Now turn on your big brains and get your trigger fingers ready!
